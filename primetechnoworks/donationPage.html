About the Campaign

In today's digital age, access to technology is not a luxury but a necessity for education. However, thousands of children in rural Maharashtra still lack basic computer skills and internet access, putting them at a severe disadvantage in their future careers.

Our campaign aims to equip 50 rural schools with fully functional computer labs. Each lab will include:

  • 10 refurbished laptops with pre-installed educational software
  • 1 year of high-speed satellite internet connectivity
  • Training materials for both students and teachers

Your contribution directly bridges the digital divide, opening up a world of knowledge and opportunities for these bright young minds.
